pig game

Pig Game is a 2 player game and the main goal is to reach to 100 points before your opponent. With rolling the dice you will collect points and you can see this points on the below 'Current'. If you draw '1', you will lose your current points, your turn will be over, will be opponent turn. To save the points below the 'Current', you should click to 'Hold'. If you click 'Hold' you will add up your points and will be opponent turn.
